Heru Setiawan

Heru Setiawan is an illustrator. He tells stories through drawings (he apparently loves it). His rough yet softly painted brushes with cozy and calm colors distinguish the look of his works. After graduating bachelor visual communication design degree from ITS Surabaya, he spends most of his time resting and making illustrations for children's books, animations, and for himself along with a small team of friends in Losmunos.

He has illustrated several children's books, including Rumah untuk Umang and Let's Go on a Hike. His latest, Ben & July and a Gift for Popo are planned to be released soon.
